Safety & Inclusivity

Taco stops are completely optional, and spice levels are designed to be fun, safe, and accessible. You do not need to declare your spiciness before the race and are welcome to change as desired. Please run within your limits.

Sliding scale between tiers

You can downgrade during the event if needed: Diablo ‚Üí Hot, Hot ‚Üí Mild. The only DNFs are for not completing the full distance.
